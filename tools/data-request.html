How to use this checklist

  1. Share with your team: Download and email this checklist to ops, finance, and planning leads with a 48-hour deadline.
  2. Gather data: Collect data from ERP, maintenance systems, crew management, revenue systems, and annual accounts.
  3. Track progress: Use the checkbox on the left to track what's collected. Progress is saved in your browser.
  4. Prioritise: Items marked PRIORITY are non-negotiable for baseline diagnostics.
  5. Validate: Cross-check data sources and flag any inconsistencies to the finance/ops leads.
  6. Hand over: Consolidate into a single folder and share with your DN engagement lead for analysis.
