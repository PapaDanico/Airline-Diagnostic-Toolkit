How to use this assessment

  1. Fill in current proficiency: Rate each competency 1–5 for your team's present state. Be honest; gaps here drive investment decisions.
  2. Confirm target proficiency: The targets above reflect best-practice standards. Adjust if your business model requires different levels (e.g., a low-cost carrier may target 3 for some customer-service competencies).
  3. Calculate gaps: Gap = Target − Current. Positive = investment needed.
  4. Prioritize: Focus on gaps that are both large (impact) and affect critical safety or revenue competencies. Flag those as high priority.
  5. Cost estimate: Typical costs: basic classroom training £100–300/person; type-rating/specialist £500–2,000/person; mentorship/on-job £50–150/person. External instructors add 30–50%.
  6. Build a roadmap: Sequence training by priority and business cycle. Use the 90-Day Sprint tool to integrate with other operational fixes.
