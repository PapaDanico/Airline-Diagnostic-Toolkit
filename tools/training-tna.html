How to use this assessment

  1. Rate current proficiency: For each competency, select your team's present level (1–5). Be honest — gaps drive investment decisions.
  2. Review the gap: Gap = Target − Current. The tool flags priority by gap size and how safety-critical the competency is.
  3. Read the priority: High = large gap on a critical competency, close first. Medium and Low follow.
  4. Use the cost estimate: Indicative per-person figures (specialist/technical training costs more than soft-skills). Multiply by headcount for a budget envelope.
  5. Build a roadmap: Sequence training by priority. Use the 90-Day Sprint tool to integrate with other operational fixes.
