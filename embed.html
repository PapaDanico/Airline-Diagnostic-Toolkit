Host site — Airline Digital Readiness

Demonstration of an embedded scorecard. (This grey chrome is the host page, not DN's.)

Embed snippet

Paste this into the host site's CMS:

<iframe
  src="https://dnconsultancydiagnostictoolkit.netlify.app/diagnostic.html"
  width="100%" height="1400" style="border:0;border-radius:12px"
  title="DN Airline Health Scorecard"></iframe>

Optional co-branding

Once a partner is registered in assets/js/data.js, add their token to the URL — for example diagnostic.html?partner=EXAMPLE — to swap in their accent colour, logo and a co-brand line. Remember to add their domain to _headers (frame-ancestors) before go-live.
